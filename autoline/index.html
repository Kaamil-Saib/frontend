What we offer

Tutoring Opitions

  • Online Tutoring

    We offer daily online lessons for all grades up to university level via Zoom, Google Classroom or Google chat .Our online lessons resume from 4:30 pm to 10pm daily. Contact us for a booking of your slot via our Whats App and email contacts to book.

    Learn More

  • Face-to-face Tutoring

    Prefer to have a tutor come to you? We would have to send a tutor to your residence to conduct the lesson. The area of your residence will have to a maximum of 15km radius from Durban CBD. Our rates will be determined by the distance travelled. Our face to face lessons may be conducted on Sundays only from 12 to 5pm.

    Learn More

  • PAT Guidance and Coaching

    We will advise, coach and assist learners on how best to approach the PAT or otherwise the Practical assessment task. The PAT involves a design process which is normally a challenge for many EGD learners and offer assistance with clearly outlining the process and assisting our learners with the brainstorming process. Our hourly rates apply.
