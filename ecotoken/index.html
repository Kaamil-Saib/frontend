Tokenomics

Eco-Shop is a community token.

Transaction fee

A 12% tax on sell transactions promote holding and rewards holders by taking away from sellers. It also ensures longevity and continued growth of our project

Liquidity pool

6% to the liquidity pool ensures a higher price floor

Burn

2% burn makes the token deflationary and reduces supply

Charity wallet

1% to the charity wallet to give back and help make this world a better place

Marketing wallet

1% to the marketing wallet ensures longevity of this project and that we will constantly have funds to market

Holders earn

2% to holders rewards diamond hands and supporters of our project for just holding
